## Want to convert your WooCommerce into a Multi vendor Marketplace ?

This plugin will help you to amazingly change your WooCommerce store into an outstanding **Multi vendor Marketplace**.

Mercado is the smartest, advance, powerful, robust and next generation end-to-end marketplace on WordPress, Powered by WooCommerce. It provides Vendors admirable frontend Dashboard for live reports of their products, orders & earnings. And for admin area there is also a stunning Dashboard which allow you to keep an eye on your ongoing store, Which will help you to create your marketplace like shopify, amazon, flipkart & ebay just after few clicks.

###  Vendor Dashboard with Live Reports
Mercado multi vendor for WooCommerce allow vendors to track their products, orders, reports, earnings & many more at their panel.

### Separate Vendor Store
Every vendor have separate store and Mercado also manages its SEO by defining its breadcrumb.

### Vendor can control its Store
There is several capabilities given to the  vendor in store-setting by which he/she can control the his/her store page UI.

### Export Order CSV
Mercado multivendor for WooCommerce allows vendors to export fresh csv for their orders, which improve interaction of vendors towards marketplace.

###  Simple, Flexible & Easy to Operate
you can simply operate and view your running store easily with few clicks, Every Operation made simple to run your marketplace on your clicks. you will experience Mercado smooth and fast operations on your store.

###  Puts Everything in control
You(Admin) can control everything that is happening in your marketplace - vendor registration, order status changes, product approval and everything which is ongoing in your marketplace.

### Key Features

#### No reload dashboard
In Mercado multivendor every vendor and admin have a no reload dashboards which makes its faster and time saving.

#### Dashboard For Vendors
  * Mercado provides dashboard for vendors to track their live status of product, report, order and earnings, seller will also get their unique store, store URL & branding name.

#### Multiple Commission
  * You(Admin) can set multiple commission for products which raise admin earnings from each and every sale.

#### Multiple Vendor in Order
  * Mercado multivendor has super intelligent action, which distribute multiple vendors order as per their price, commission and earning. It amazingly manage orders which is from more than 1 vendor. Customer also  get access to track his order according to the vendors.

#### Withdrawals with status tracking
  * Vendor can create requests for withdrawals from available payment in their wallets. which requests will be approved or cancelled by you(Admin).

#### Add Payment to Vendor's Wallet on your Flexibility
  * You(Admin) can send payment in vendors wallet as your flexibility(On order completion, After you approve  & on other order  status).

#### Seller's Store Listing
  * You as an Admin can view all your running stores with their details at one place of your marketplace.

#### Separate commission for each vendor
  * You as an Admin can set different commissions for different-different vendors as per their performance.

#### Simple Product & Order Management
  * You(Admin) can simply manage product publishing status, product commission, create product, assign product to particular vendor.
  * You(Admin) can simply manage orders by changing order status and also can delete orders.

#### Google Map & Mapbox API
  * Two way Google API Integration - which allow you to display Google Map & Mapbox for your marketplace.

#### Ajax Searching, Sorting & Filtering
  * Mercado provides you outstanding sorting, filtering & searching features which reduce your time and help you to manage your marketplace in minutes, as well as your sellers can also take benefits of these features.

#### Security Aspects
 * Every vendor can only see his/her order, product and Media files .When vendor uploads a media/file Mercado ensures that no other than admin can see that media/file.

#### Mercado Settings for each and everything

From Settings you(Admin) can customise everything in your marketplace.

* You can manage vendors URL, vendors access, vendors capabilities, wizard logo, store terms and conditions.
* In other step you can also manage order status at the time of accepting order, commission type and values.
* You can set withdrawals option for vendors with minimum amount limit.
* Custom page setting option to set your pages wherever you want.
* Vendor's store time widgets, contact form, map enabling with privacy & policy & many more setting for customising your store as per your need.

== Installation ==

= Minimum Requirements =

* WordPress 5.0 or greater
* PHP version 5x or greater
* MySQL version 5.0 or greater

= We recommend your host supports =

* PHP version 5x or greater
* MySQL version 5.6 or greater
* WordPress Memory limit of 64 MB or greater (128 MB or higher is preferred)

= Automatic installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option as WordPress handles the file transfers itself and you don't need to leave your web browser. To do an automatic install of Mercado, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu and click Add New.

In the search field type "Mercado" and click Search Plugins. Once you've found our Mercado you can view details about it such as the point release, rating and description. Most importantly of course, you can install it by simply clicking "Install Now".

= Manual installation =

The manual installation method involves downloading our Mercado and uploading it to your web server via your favorite FTP application. The WordPress codex contains [instructions on how to do this here](http://codex.wordpress.org/Managing_Plugins#Manual_Plugin_Installation).
